The Journey

Beneath the starlit sky so deep,
Where whispered dreams and secrets sleep,
A wanderer treads the winding road,
With hope as compass, heart as code.

The mountains rise with peaks so high,
They pierce the canvas of the sky,
Each step forward, though tired and slow,
Reminds us all of how we grow.

In valleys green where rivers flow,
The gentle winds begin to blow,
They carry tales of distant lands,
Of footprints left in golden sands.

The journey's not about the end,
But every lesson that we mend,
Each stumble teaches, every fall,
A stepping stone to standing tall.

So when the path seems dark and long,
Remember that you must be strong,
For in the quest, we find our way,
And turn the night into the day.
